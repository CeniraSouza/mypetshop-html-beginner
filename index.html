About us

Our shop first opened its doors in 1970. Bill was the local Vet and decided to provide, in the same place, veterinary services, animal adoption, convenience store for animal food, accessories and medicine. These days Bill's family is also responsible for running My Petshop. John and Lucas are our Vets and they love animals. Brigitte is our manager and is always happy to help.
